Restaurant Management System

Project Date February 9th, 2024
Visit https://github.com/uzairsaeedi/Restaurant-Management-System-in-C-Desktop-Application-.git

The Restaurant Management System is a C-based desktop application designed to streamline restaurant operations, including order management, billing, and inventory tracking. The system provides an intuitive interface for restaurant staff to manage customer orders efficiently, track menu items, and generate bills, making the entire process more organized and hassle-free.

Key Features:

  • Take New Orders: Staff can input and manage customer orders with ease.
  • Table Management: Assign orders to specific tables and track their status.
  • Add, Update, & Remove Menu Items: Admins can manage the restaurantâ€™s menu dynamically.
  • Search Feature: Quickly find menu items by name or category.
  • Automated Bill Generation: Calculates total price, taxes, and discounts automatically.
  • Print Invoice:Generates and prints receipts for customers.
